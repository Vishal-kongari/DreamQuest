Engineering Career Path

Engineering is a field that involves designing, building, and maintaining systems, structures, and processes. It encompasses multiple disciplines, such as mechanical, electrical, civil, and computer engineering. Engineers use their knowledge of science, mathematics, and technology to solve real-world problems.

How to Achieve a Career in Engineering:

  • 1. Complete high school with a strong focus on mathematics and science subjects.
  • 2. Pursue a Bachelor’s degree in Engineering or a related field (such as Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, etc.).
  • 3. Gain hands-on experience through internships, workshops, and projects.
  • 4. Obtain professional certifications and licenses depending on the specific engineering discipline.
  • 5. Explore advanced education (Master’s or PhD) if aiming for research or leadership positions.
